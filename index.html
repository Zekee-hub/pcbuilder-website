FAQ

Why do you require 100% upfront payment?

We’re a boutique builder. Upfront payment lets us order your components immediately without carrying inventory costs—so you get better prices.

Can you ship the PC?

Not at the moment. We operate pickup-only to eliminate shipping risk and cost. That’s part of how we keep prices tight.

How long does a build take?

Typically 5–10 working days from payment, depending on part availability and complexity.

Do you offer warranties?

Yes. 1-year labour warranty from us, plus full manufacturer warranties on parts.

Can I watch the build process?

We’ll send you progress photos/videos. If you want a full build video, we can price that in.
